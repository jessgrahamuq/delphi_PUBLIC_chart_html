7.3 Lack of capability or robustness - Optional Actors Responsibility Assessment

Higher Responsibility

Summary of expert comments: Comments varied on specialized developers' responsibility, with most emphasizing higher responsibility than GPAI developers because specific use cases make it easier to assess capabilities and inform users. Multiple responses noted high responsibility due to domain-specific risks in healthcare, law, and safety-critical fields where robustness is not optional with higher standards required. However, one comment suggested specialized developers using GPT wrappers are no different from end users regarding unreliable AI systems.

Higher Responsibility

Summary of expert comments: Comments consistently rated infrastructure providers as highly to moderately responsible, with computational limitations and design choices affecting system reliability. Responses noted they enable scaling and can require minimal robustness or testing standards as part of access conditions, with it being their job to maximize robustness. Comments highlighted data custodians face high vulnerability if storage corruption or version mismatch introduces misalignment during training.
