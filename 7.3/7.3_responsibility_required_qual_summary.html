7.3 Lack of capability or robustness - Responsibility

Higher Responsibility

Summary of expert comments: Comments overwhelmingly emphasized GPAI developers' primary responsibility as they design core architectures influencing robustness across applications, with robustness needing to be designed at model level not just patched downstream. Multiple responses noted developers carry most responsibility as their foundational work sets the tone for system reliability, with failures cascading across sectors if robustness isn't embedded from the start. Comments stressed developers are the main party capable of ensuring efficacy, with it being their job to maximize robustness.
